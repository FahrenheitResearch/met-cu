  WORKLOAD SPECIFICATION
  --------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Model:           HRRR (High-Resolution Rapid Refresh)
  Grid:            1059 x 1799 (3-km CONUS Lambert Conformal)
  Columns/grid:    1,905,141
  Vertical levels: 40 isobaric (1013 - 50 hPa)
  Temporal scope:  1 calendar year (365 days)
  Init frequency:  24 cycles/day (hourly)
  Forecast hours:  00/06/12/18z: f00-f48 (49 hrs)
                   All others:   f00-f18 (19 hrs)
  Grids/day:       576
  Grids/year:      210,240
  Total soundings: 400,536,843,840 (~400.5 billion)

  COMPUTATION PER GRID
  --------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Full surface-based parcel profile (RK4 moist adiabat, 10-hPa substep)
  CAPE/CIN integration (LFC-bounded, virtual temperature corrected)
  LCL, LFC, EL detection with interpolated crossings
  Mixed-layer and most-unstable parcel variants
  Precipitable water (trapezoidal, native levels)
  0-1, 0-3, 0-6 km bulk wind shear
  0-1, 0-3 km storm-relative helicity
  Bunkers right/left mover storm motion
  STP, SCP, SHIP, EHI, DCP composites
  K-Index, Total Totals, SWEAT, Showalter, Lifted Index
  Relative vorticity, divergence, frontogenesis (2D stencil)
  Gaussian smoothing (sigma=3)

  HARDWARE
  --------------------------------------------------------------------------
  GPU:  NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090
        Blackwell architecture, 170 SMs, 21,760 CUDA cores
        32 GB GDDR7, 1,792 GB/s bandwidth
        CUDA 13.0, Driver 580.97
  CPU:  Reference comparison on 16-core x86_64
        metrust v0.3.8 (Rust, compiled with LTO)
        rayon work-stealing parallelism

  KERNEL IMPLEMENTATION
  --------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Framework:       CuPy 14.0.1 (RawKernel + ElementwiseKernel)
  Precision:       float64 throughout (no mixed-precision)
  Column kernels:  1 CUDA thread per sounding column
  Stencil kernels: (16,16) thread blocks, centered differences
  Per-element:     Fused single-pass kernels, zero intermediate alloc
  Moist adiabat:   RK4 integration of Bakhshaii-Stull lapse rate
  CAPE formula:    g * (Tv_parcel - Tv_env) / Tv_env * dz
  CIN bounding:    Last negative-to-positive crossing (true LFC)
  Total kernels:   145 hand-written CUDA kernels
